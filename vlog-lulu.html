LuLu Techpark: Shaping the Future of Kerala's IT Landscape

The world-class IT parks in Kochi are the cornerstone of Kerala's rapid rise to prominence as one of India's most promising technology hubs. Built to serve the expanding needs of global enterprises, startups, and tech innovators, these parks seamlessly integrate premium infrastructure, flexible office solutions, and a dynamic business community.

A New Benchmark in Workspaces

LuLu IT Parks has emerged as a major landmark in the technological landscape of Kerala. Strategically located in Smart City-Kochi and Infopark, these state-of-the-art campuses are designed for global IT standards.

With an astounding 3.5 million sq. ft. of built-up area and 2.5 million sq. ft. of leasable space, LuLu Techpark accommodates multinational corporations and innovators under one roof. The facility offers everything necessary for smooth operations, from redundant high-speed fiber-optic networks to uninterrupted power supply systems.

World-Class Lifestyle and Amenities

What sets LuLu Techpark apart is its focus on creating an integrated convenience hub. It features the world's largest robotic parking facility, capable of accommodating 4,500 vehicles. Professionals working here enjoy a gourmet food court, wellness amenities, grand lobby spaces, and beautifully designed green landscapes.

By successfully attracting multinational corporations, LuLu IT Parks is accelerating the economic momentum in Kochi. For companies seeking managed office spaces, it presents a matchless blend of design brilliance, eco-friendliness, and ease of operations—setting a new standard for the modern workplace in Kerala.
